In the 21st century, women, non-binary, and gender non-conforming people struggle with maintaining and knowing their rights.

Our website is made to advocate for women, non-binary, and gender non-conforming people by recognizing the amazing achievements they have accomplished, as well as bring their struggles in their daily lives and professions to light.
